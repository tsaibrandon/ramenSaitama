About Us

Welcome to Ramen Saitama, where we've been crafting authentic Japanese ramen since 2001. Our journey began with a simple passion for bringing traditional Japanese flavors to the heart of America.

Our founder, Chef Takashi, learned the art of ramen-making in his hometown in Japan. After decades of perfecting his craft, he brought his family recipes to Los Angeles, where Ramen Saitama was born.

At Ramen Saitama, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients. Our broths are simmered for over 24 hours to extract maximum flavor, and our noodles are made fresh daily. We take pride in creating a dining experience that transports you straight to the bustling streets of Tokyo.

From our humble beginnings in Little Tokyo, we've grown to multiple locations across California and Nevada, but our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged.

We invite you to come experience our passion for ramen and Japanese cuisine. Whether you're a ramen enthusiast or trying it for the first time, we promise an unforgettable culinary journey.
